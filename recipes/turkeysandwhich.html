Turkey Sandwhich

Description

This is the most average turkey sandwhich that you will ever see.

Ingrediants

  • sliced turkey from the deli
  • rye bread
  • mayo
  • lettuce/tomatoe (optional)

Steps

  1. place your sliced tukey in-between the two slices on rye bread
  2. spread mayo evenly on one side of the rye bread
  3. (optional) place lettuce and tomatoe on same side as mayo
  4. close the bread, cut in half diagonally, enjoy!
